Coffee Show

The design of the coffee shop Oak and Ivy will be based on the ideas of timeless and wellness. To create a comfortable and inviting space for both tourists and people working remotely, this design will feature a color palette of neutrals accented by pops of color. Limewashed walls and checkered terra cotta floors will be incorporated to create movement and color. Casework and detailed finish work will be added to the walls to reflect the rich history of the Financial District of New York City. The concept of wellness will be included through the wood elements, plants, natural light but mostly through the menu. Regular coffee and lattes will be served along with mushroom coffee and Cacao that incorporates adaptogens . Smoothies with the option of additives will also be served, creating a unique menu with options for a variety of guests.
