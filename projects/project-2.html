Hospital

The ultimate drive behind this project is patient-centered care. This is accomplished by respecting privacy of patients while also giving them and their families the accommodations needed so they feel empowered. This design will also be accomplished through a comfortable environment that supports healing and the staff goals. Aesthetically this will be achieved through organic shapes, soft furnishings, greenery, abstract art, and wood tones. To make this ASC comfortable it should imitate a home or spa. Through warm tones and daylighting this space will be comfortable, promote healing, and produce patient-centered care.
