Design Statement and Resume


As a designer, I believe every space holds the potential for beauty, and my passion is to bring that beauty to life. I focus on creating spaces that are not only functional and user-centered but also deeply personal, comfortable, and inspiring. My approach incorporates architectural details and sustainable practices to craft designs that stand the test of time and promote well-being.I have always appreciated the beauty of physical art, but I find my creativity best expressed through architectural details—custom wall designs, elegant arches, and intricate casework that bring character to a space.Central to my work is my faith, as I strive to reflect the Creator in my designs. Inspired by nature’s balance and beauty, I incorporate elements like greenery, wood, and natural palettes to honor God’s creation. My goal is to create spaces that inspire, uplift, and reflect His creativity.
